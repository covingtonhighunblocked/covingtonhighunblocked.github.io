How to Play

Im adding this just to give yall somewhat of an idea how to play the game

The first thing you want to do is to damage the block in the middle of the screen, which you can do by clicking, or by pressing space. By default you do 1 damage, and the block always has 10 health

After 10 damage, the block will break, and the item will appear in your inventory, the farthest left list on screen, which will display how many you own when you hover over an object

Once you have enough items, you can craft new items using the three other lists. These items can give you damage bonuses and idle damage. They can also be purchases as many times as you want currently

If a list has enough items in it, scroll to see all options

So far, thats all there is to know. I hope you enjoy!
